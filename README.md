# Maritime AI Intelligence Platform

## Overview

Maritime AI Intelligence Platform is an enterprise-grade decision support system designed for ports, shipping lines, terminal operators, freight forwarders, and maritime stakeholders.

The platform consolidates vessel intelligence, voyage risk monitoring, port congestion analytics, business intelligence, and competitor port benchmarking into a single executive dashboard.

---

* Port congestion intelligence
